To use application:

1) click "take image"
2) rotate iPad counterclockwise and take image of left side of map
3) click "take image" again
4) rotate iPad again and take image of right side of map
5) click "Stitch". Wait for response
6) click "Threshold"
7) click "Contours"
8) click "Warp"
9) click "Analyze"
